Toyed around and am decently satisfied with this: a, .link   &[rel^="external"],   &[rel*=" external"],   &[target^="blank"],   &[target*="blank "],   &:not([href*="website.com"]),   &:not([href^="/"]),   &:not([href^="#"]),   &.external {     //external styles   } } Some other methods are here.  Do with it what you'd like.
